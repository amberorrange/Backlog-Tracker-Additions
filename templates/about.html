About Backlog Tracker


Backlog Tracker is an app for keeping track of a your video game backlog. You can add games to your backlog, add reviews for games you've played, and keep data on your gameplay, such as the number of hours per genre played or genres per platform played on. I hope you enjoy this personal project.
